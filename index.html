    Marriage Registration Guidance & Services

    To register marriages done after March 27, 2010, comply with Uttarakhand government rules by providing necessary documents and adhere to all regulations for valid marriage certification.

    • At least one spouse must be a permanent resident of Uttarakhand.
    • Valid residence or age certificate from either spouse.
    • Both husband and wife Aadhaar cards are required.
    • Both spouses must have PAN cards.
    • Provide Ayushman Card if available.
    • Provide marriage receipt in case of Muslim marriage registration.
    • Birth certificate of children (if any) at the time of registration.
    • Both spouses' Aadhaar linked mobile numbers must be provided.
    • Photos of bride, groom, and both spouses together during registration.
    • Presence of bride and groom is mandatory at registration.
